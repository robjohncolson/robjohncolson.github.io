Thai Green Papaya Salad (Som Tum)

adapted from Pailin Chongchitnant's ส้มตำไทย

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups julienned green papaya
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 6 Thai chilies
  • 1 ½ Tbsp turbinado sugar
  • 1 Tbsp peanut butter
  • 1/2 TB fermented shrimp paste.
  • 1 Lime
  • 1 TB tamarind paste
  • 1 ½ Tbsp fish sauce
  • 6 cherry tomatoes
  • 2 baby bamboo shoots

Instructions

  1. soak tamarind in just enough water to soften
  2. chop garlic and chili
  3. add sugar
  4. in a separate mixing bowl (preferably nonmetal) put papaya
  5. slice bamboo shoots into strips and put in mixing bowl
  6. cut cherry tomatoes in half and put in mixing bowl.
  7. put the chili-sugar-garlic, tamarind + water, peanutbutter, fish sauce, lime juice, shrimp paste into mixing bowl
  8. mix, salt to taste, serve
