Thai Green Papaya Salad (Som Tum)

adapted from Pailin Chongchitnant's ส้มตำไทย

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups julienned green papaya
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 6 Thai chilies
  • 3 Tbsp coconut sugar
  • 1 Tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 tsp fermented shrimp paste.
  • 2 Lime
  • 2 TB tamarind paste
  • 1 ½ Tbsp fish sauce
  • 6 cherry tomatoes
  • 2 baby bamboo shoots
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp chili flak

Instructions

  1. soak tamarind in just enough water to soften
  2. Add 3 TB of coconut sugar to tamarind
  3. place shredded papaya in a separate mixing bowl
  4. slice bamboo shoots into strips and put in mixing bowl
  5. cut cherry tomatoes in half and put in mixing bowl
  6. smash garlic, then roughly chop. Add to mixing bowl
  7. cut chilies in half, then roughly chop. Add to mixing bowl
  8. grate lime skins into mixing bowl
  9. slice limes in half and squeeze juice into mixing bowl
  10. add salt, chili flake, peanut butter and tamarind mixture to mixing bowl
  11. put all ingredients away, and prepare serving bowls
  12. for whoever does not like the funk, set aside some salad for them
  13. In the bottom of the serving bowl, add shrimp paste
  14. From the mixing bowl, transfer papaya salad to serving bowl
  15. Finally, add fish sauce
  16. eat
